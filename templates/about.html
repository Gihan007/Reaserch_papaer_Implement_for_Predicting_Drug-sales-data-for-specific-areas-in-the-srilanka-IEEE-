About Our Research

Cutting-edge AI for pharmaceutical sales forecasting in Sri Lanka

IEEE Research Project

Predicting Drug Sales Data for Specific Areas in Sri Lanka

This research project implements advanced machine learning and deep learning techniques to forecast pharmaceutical sales across different regions in Sri Lanka. Our system combines traditional statistical methods with cutting-edge AI approaches to provide accurate, interpretable, and uncertainty-aware predictions.

Research Contributions

10+ ML Models

Comprehensive comparison of traditional and deep learning approaches

Meta-Learning

Learn-to-learn across drug categories with few-shot adaptation

Uncertainty Quantification

Bayesian methods for reliable decision-making

Machine Learning Models

Traditional Models
  • LightGBM - Gradient boosting framework
  • XGBoost - Extreme Gradient Boosting
  • Prophet - Time series forecasting by Facebook
  • SARIMAX - Seasonal ARIMA with exogenous variables
Deep Learning Models
  • LSTM - Long Short-Term Memory networks
  • GRU - Gated Recurrent Units
  • Transformer - Attention-based architecture
  • Temporal Fusion Transformer (TFT)
  • N-BEATS - Neural basis expansion analysis

Advanced Features

Meta-Learning
  • MAML - Model-Agnostic Meta-Learning
  • Few-shot Learning - Adapt with minimal data
  • Transfer Learning - Cross-category knowledge transfer
Interpretability & Uncertainty
  • SHAP Values - Feature importance explanations
  • Counterfactual Analysis - What-if scenarios
  • Bayesian Neural Networks - Uncertainty estimation
  • Monte Carlo Dropout - Prediction intervals
  • Conformal Prediction - Guaranteed coverage
